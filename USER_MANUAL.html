üéØ Introduction

Welcome to the Legal RAG System, an advanced legal assistance platform powered by artificial intelligence. This system combines cutting-edge AI technology with a comprehensive legal knowledge base to provide intelligent document analysis, case management, and legal research capabilities.

What is RAG?

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) is an AI architecture that combines the power of large language models with a searchable knowledge base. When you ask a question, the system:

  1. Searches through thousands of legal documents to find relevant information
  2. Retrieves the most pertinent passages
  3. Uses GPT-4 to generate accurate, contextualized answers based on actual legal texts
Key Benefits:
  • Accurate answers grounded in real legal documents
  • Faster legal research than traditional methods
  • Access to comprehensive legal knowledge base
  • Natural language interaction - no legal jargon required

ü§ñ AI Query System

The AI Query System leverages GPT-4 and RAG technology to provide intelligent answers based on your documents and the legal knowledge base.

How It Works

The RAG Process

  1. You Ask: Type your legal question in natural language
  2. System Searches: AI searches through thousands of legal documents
  3. Relevant Content Retrieved: Most pertinent passages are identified
  4. AI Generates Answer: GPT-4 creates a comprehensive answer with citations

Making Queries

Basic Query

Step 1: Navigate to AI Query page
Step 2: Type your question

Example: "What are the requirements for a valid contract in commercial law?"

Step 3: Select search scope (optional)
  • All documents
  • Specific case only
  • Legal knowledge base only
  • Your uploaded documents only
Step 4: Click Ask AI
Step 5: Review the answer

The AI will provide a detailed response with source citations

Query Best Practices

Tips for Better Results:
  • Be specific in your questions
  • Include relevant context (jurisdiction, case type, etc.)
  • Ask one question at a time
  • Use legal terminology when appropriate
  • Reference specific documents if needed

Good Query Examples

Poor Query Better Query
"What about contracts?" "What are the essential elements required for a valid commercial contract?"
"Tell me about this case" "What are the key legal issues in Smith vs. Jones contract dispute case?"
"Laws about property" "What are the legal requirements for property transfer in residential real estate?"

Understanding AI Responses

Response Components

  • Answer: Direct response to your question
  • Sources: Legal documents and passages used
  • Confidence Score: AI's confidence in the answer (0-100%)
  • Related Topics: Suggested follow-up questions
  • Citations: Specific references with page numbers

Confidence Levels

Score Meaning Action
90-100% High confidence Answer well-supported by documents
70-89% Good confidence Answer supported, consider verifying
50-69% Moderate confidence Review sources carefully, may need more context
Below 50% Low confidence Limited information available, verify independently

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes. We use enterprise-grade encryption for data at rest and in transit. All documents are stored securely, and access is logged for security auditing. We comply with GDPR and other data protection regulations.

Q: Can I use the system offline?

A: No, Legal RAG System requires an internet connection to access AI features and cloud-stored documents. However, you can download documents for offline viewing.

Q: What languages are supported?

A: Currently, the system supports Spanish and English for documents and queries. We plan to add more languages in future updates.

Subscription Questions

Q: Can I change plans anytime?

A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated.

Q: What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

A: You'll receive notifications when approaching limits. If exceeded, you can upgrade your plan or wait until the next billing period. We'll never charge you without notification.

Q: Do you offer refunds?

A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers. If you're not satisfied, contact support within 30 days of your first payment.

Technical Questions

Q: How accurate is the AI?

A: Our AI uses GPT-4, which provides highly accurate responses based on the documents in the system. However, AI responses should be reviewed by legal professionals and not considered as legal advice.

Q: Can I integrate with other legal software?

A: Team plan subscribers have access to our API for custom integrations. Contact sales for integration possibilities.

Q: How often is the legal knowledge base updated?

A: Our legal knowledge base is updated continuously as new laws, regulations, and jurisprudence are published. Updates typically occur weekly.

Privacy Questions

Q: Who can see my documents?

A: Only you and users you explicitly share with can access your documents. Our staff cannot view your documents except when required for technical support with your explicit permission.

Q: Is my query history private?

A: Yes, all queries and their results are private to your account. We use anonymized, aggregated data for system improvements but never share individual query data.

Q: Can I delete my data?

A: Yes, you can delete individual documents, cases, or your entire account. Deletion is permanent and typically completed within 30 days.

Support Questions

Q: Do you offer training?

A: Professional and Team plan subscribers receive onboarding training. Team plan includes custom training sessions for your organization.

Q: Is there a mobile app?

A: The web interface is mobile-responsive. Native mobile apps for iOS and Android are in development and will be released in 2025.

Q: Can I import cases from other systems?

A: We offer data migration assistance for Team plan subscribers. Contact our support team to discuss your migration needs.
