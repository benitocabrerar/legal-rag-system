## Introduction

Welcome to the Natural Language Search system for Ecuadorian legal documents. This guide will help you search for laws, decrees, regulations, and other legal documents using everyday Spanish language, without needing to know complex search syntax or legal codes.

### What Can You Search For?

- **Laws (Leyes)**: Constitutional, organic, and ordinary laws
- **Codes (Códigos)**: Civil, penal, labor, tax, and other codes
- **Decrees (Decretos)**: Presidential and executive decrees
- **Regulations (Reglamentos)**: Implementing regulations
- **Resolutions (Resoluciones)**: Administrative decisions
- **Ordinances (Ordenanzas)**: Municipal regulations
- **Articles and Provisions**: Specific legal provisions

### How It Works

1. **Type your question** in natural Spanish
2. **AI analyzes** your query to understand what you're looking for
3. **Search filters** are automatically created
4. **Results** are ranked by relevance
5. **Recommendations** help you refine your search

---

## Getting Started

### Basic Search

Simply type what you're looking for in plain Spanish:

```
leyes laborales vigentes
```

The system will:
- Identify you're looking for **laws** (not decrees or resolutions)
- Recognize the **labor law** topic
- Filter for **current/active** laws only

### Adding More Details

Be more specific to get better results:

```
leyes laborales vigentes sobre contratos de trabajo del 2023
```

Now the system also knows:
- You want laws from **2023**
- Specifically about **employment contracts**
- Still looking for **active** laws

### Asking Questions

You can also phrase your search as a question:

```
¿Qué dice la ley sobre despidos injustificados?
```

The system understands:
- You're asking about **law** (not regulations)
- Topic is **unjustified dismissals** (labor law)
- You want **substantive content** (not just references)

## Query Patterns

### Pattern 1: Norm Type + Topic

**Format:** `[tipo de norma] + [tema]`

**Examples:**
- "leyes laborales"
- "decretos ambientales"
- "resoluciones tributarias"
- "ordenanzas de tránsito"

**What the system extracts:**
- Norm type (ley, decreto, etc.)
- Legal topic (laboral, ambiental, etc.)

---

### Pattern 2: Norm Type + Topic + Time

**Format:** `[tipo de norma] + [tema] + [tiempo]`

**Examples:**
- "leyes laborales de 2023"
- "decretos ambientales del último año"
- "resoluciones del SRI entre enero y junio 2024"
- "ordenanzas vigentes"

**What the system extracts:**
- Norm type
- Legal topic
- Date range or validity status

---

### Pattern 3: Specific Law/Code + Article

**Format:** `[nombre de la ley/código] + artículo(s)`

**Examples:**
- "Código Civil artículos sobre contratos"
- "COIP artículo 140"
- "Constitución artículo 66"
- "LOSEP reglamento artículo 25"

**What the system extracts:**
- Specific law/code name
- Article number (if provided)
- Topic area

---

### Pattern 4: Entity + Topic

**Format:** `[entidad emisora] + [tema]`

**Examples:**
- "Ministerio de Trabajo sobre teletrabajo"
- "SRI sobre facturación electrónica"
- "Presidencia sobre estado de excepción"
- "Asamblea Nacional sobre educación"

**What the system extracts:**
- Issuing entity
- Topic
- Jurisdiction (inferred)

---

### Pattern 5: Geographic Scope + Norm Type

**Format:** `[ámbito geográfico] + [tipo de norma]`

**Examples:**
- "ordenanzas de Quito sobre transporte"
- "leyes provinciales de Pichincha"
- "normativa municipal de Cuenca"
- "decretos nacionales vigentes"

**What the system extracts:**
- Geographic scope
- Jurisdiction level
- Norm type

---

### Pattern 6: Question Format

**Format:** `¿[pregunta en lenguaje natural]?`

**Examples:**
- "¿Qué dice la ley sobre despidos?"
- "¿Cuáles son los derechos del trabajador?"
- "¿Cómo funciona el hábeas corpus?"
- "¿Cuándo prescribe una deuda tributaria?"

**What the system extracts:**
- Query intent (find provision, understand procedure, check validity)
- Legal topic
- Relevant norm types

## Tips for Better Results

### 1. Be Specific About What You Want

❌ **Too vague:** "leyes"
✅ **Better:** "leyes laborales vigentes sobre contratos"

Why? The system can better identify:
- **Subject area** (labor law)
- **Status** (current/active)
- **Specific topic** (contracts)

---

### 2. Include Time References When Relevant

❌ **Missing context:** "decretos sobre educación"
✅ **Better:** "decretos sobre educación de 2023"

Why? Focuses results on:
- **Recent regulations**
- **Specific time period**
- **Avoids obsolete norms**

---

### 3. Specify Jurisdiction When Needed

❌ **Unclear scope:** "regulaciones de tránsito"
✅ **Better:** "ordenanzas de tránsito de Quito"

Why? Distinguishes between:
- **National laws**
- **Provincial regulations**
- **Municipal ordinances**

---

### 4. Use Official Names When Known

❌ **Generic:** "ley de servicio público"
✅ **Better:** "Ley Orgánica de Servicio Público" or "LOSEP"

Why? Direct matching to:
- **Specific legislation**
- **Official documents**
- **Higher confidence**

---

### 5. State Your Intent Clearly

❌ **Unclear:** "contrato de trabajo"
✅ **Better (depending on intent):**
- "artículos sobre contratos de trabajo" (find provisions)
- "contratos de trabajo vigentes" (find active norms)
- "¿cómo terminar un contrato de trabajo?" (understand procedure)

---

### 6. Combine Multiple Criteria

**Good query structure:**
```
[tipo de norma] + [tema] + [estado] + [fecha]
```

**Example:**
```
leyes laborales vigentes sobre despidos del 2023
```

This gives the system:
- Norm type: ley
- Topic: laboral, despidos
- Status: vigente
- Date: 2023

---

### 7. Use Legal Terminology

❌ **Colloquial:** "me echaron del trabajo"
✅ **Legal:** "despido injustificado"

❌ **Common:** "permiso de construcción"
✅ **Legal:** "licencia de construcción" or "permiso urbanístico"

Why? Better entity recognition and topic matching.

---

### 8. Check Spelling

The system has spell-check, but correct spelling improves:
- **Confidence scores**
- **Processing speed**
- **Result accuracy**

Common corrections:
- "prescripcion" → "prescripción"
- "tramite" → "trámite"
- "codigo" → "código"

## Understanding Results

### Confidence Score

Every search shows a **confidence score** (0-100%):

- **90-100%**: Excellent - System very confident about understanding your query
- **70-89%**: Good - Results likely match your intent
- **50-69%**: Fair - Consider refining your query
- **Below 50%**: Low - Try rephrasing or adding details

**Example:**

```
Query: "leyes laborales vigentes sobre contratos de trabajo"
Confidence: 87%
```

This means the system is quite confident it understood:
- You want **laws** (not regulations or decrees)
- About **labor law**
- Specifically **employment contracts**
- That are **currently active**

---

### Entities Detected

Shows what legal concepts the system recognized:

**Example:**

If important entities are **missing**, try:

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: No Results Found

**Possible Causes:**

1. **Query too specific**
   - Solution: Remove some filters, use more general terms

2. **Spelling errors**
   - Solution: Check spelling, the system will suggest corrections

3. **Non-existent combination**
   - Solution: Verify if the combination makes sense legally

**Example:**

❌ **Too specific:** "ordenanzas nacionales de tránsito de Quito de 2023"
- Problem: Ordinances are municipal, not national
- ✅ **Better:** "ordenanzas de tránsito de Quito de 2023"

---

### Problem: Too Many Results

**Possible Causes:**

1. **Query too general**
   - Solution: Add more specific filters

2. **Missing time constraints**
   - Solution: Add date range or "vigentes"

**Example:**

❌ **Too general:** "leyes"
- Returns: Thousands of laws

✅ **Better:** "leyes laborales vigentes sobre contratos"
- Returns: Focused, relevant results

---

### Problem: Low Confidence Score

**Possible Causes:**

1. **Ambiguous query**
   - Solution: Be more specific about norm type and topic

2. **Mixed concepts**
   - Solution: Focus on one main topic

3. **Unclear intent**
   - Solution: Rephrase as a more direct question or statement

**Example:**

❌ **Ambiguous:** "cosas de trabajo"
- Confidence: 35%

✅ **Clear:** "leyes sobre contratos de trabajo"
- Confidence: 89%

---

### Problem: Wrong Type of Documents

**Possible Causes:**

1. **System misidentified norm type**
   - Solution: Explicitly state norm type

**Example:**

Query: "sobre educación"
Result: Returns laws, decrees, resolutions (mixed)

✅ **Better:** "decretos sobre educación"
Result: Returns only decrees

---

### Problem: Results Not Relevant

**What to Check:**

1. **Review detected entities**
   - Are the right concepts being recognized?

2. **Check applied filters**
   - Do they match your intent?

3. **Look at recommendations**
   - System may suggest refinements

**Example:**

If searching for "contratos" returns too much about "contratos de trabajo" but you want "contratos civiles":

✅ **Better:** "Código Civil sobre contratos" or "contratos civiles"
